As a high-end boutique custom print shop we are here to make sure that you have the best possible interaction with our team and that you receive the highest quality product possible. Getting started with BCCO NYC is simple and easy!

We welcome you to give us a call to discuss all the details of your project. Once we answer any questions you may have, you will be directed to the quote form that is available online. This is where you can lay out all of the information we need to build your quote.

Any form that is submitted before 5:00 PM will receive a same-day response. All quote requests received after this time will be answered the following business day.

If you have any questions about the quote form or would like assistance as you fill it out please feel free to give us a call and we will happily walk you through it.

We will then email you to confirm the details of your request and provide you with pricing. We will ask you to confirm the price quote before moving forward. Once confirmed we will build you an invoice. All invoices must be paid in full before we start your project.

We will then send you a mock up for your approval. Mock ups are digital representations of your design and are not necessarily to scale. All mockups will have written specifications that our production team will follow. We will never start production on an order until we receive your approval.

Once approval is received, a first run sample is made. With limited exceptions, we will render a pre-production sample for your order. We will send you a high-resolution photo of the sample and give you the option to either come in and approve it in person, or have it shipped to you. We do this to make sure that we exceed your expectations on every order you place with us. We highly recommend you approve a physical sample, however, if for any reason you wish to approve your sample based solely on the photo we send, we are happy to move forward as soon as you give us the okay. Rush orders notwithstanding, turnaround time will start once sample approval is confirmed.

We will notify you as soon as your order is ready for pick up. If your order gets shipping we will send you the tracking information as soon as it is available.

If you have any questions about the order process here at BCCO NYC please feel free to give us a call!
